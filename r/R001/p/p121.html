Figure 5.5: Fish Landing Sites in Trinidad and Tobago (Source: www.ima.gov.tt/CEPintro.html)

The Fisheries Division of the MALMR, estimated annual landings at some important landing sites in Trinidad over the period 1998 to 2004. Total catch varied over this period with the smallest catch of 3,612 tonnes registered in 2000 and the largest in 2004 with 4,816 tonnes. The total fish catch for the entire period was estimated at 31,622 tonnes of which Grand Chemin accounted for approximately 19% and was the site with the single largest catch annually for six of the seven years. Table 5.3 shows estimated landings by sites and selected years for Trinidad. In the case of Tobago, available data for 2005 show Pigeon Point and Buccoo Point as the two main landing sites accounting for 48% and 22% of Tobago total annual fish catch of 81.1 tonnes, respectively (Table 5.4).
