7.2.1 Pressure There are a number of pressures which degrade, fragment and destroy ecosystems in Trinidad and Tobago. Among the major pressures are the expansion of human settlement and continued urbanisation, forest degradation, the incidence of forest fires, pollution, and the physical alteration of ecosystems. Urbanisation is discussed in Chapter Four of this report.
