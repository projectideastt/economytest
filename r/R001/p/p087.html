Land use has been altered from agriculture to mining, and degradation of the land and siltation of nearby water courses have had deleterious effects on the adjacent agricultural communities.

The demand for quarry material is likely to continue at least in the short to medium term, given the level of construction activities taking place in the country. Indeed, the draft policy predicted that local quarry production will grow fourfold by 2020 (Table 4.7). Applications to the EMA for Certificates of Environmental Clearance (CECs) between 2001 and 2005 indicate intensification in land development. Between 2001 and 2005, 486 CEC applications for land development were received, with 159 received in 2005. Thus, roughly one third of all applications for the period was received in 2005.
